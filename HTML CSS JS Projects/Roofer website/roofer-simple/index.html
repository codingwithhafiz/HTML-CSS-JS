Who We Are?

Roofers

They are second generation roofers and lifelong residents of Elora, ON. The brothers took over their father’s business in 2008 and have quickly become one of the leading roofing contractors in the province of Ontario.

Their goal for every job is to exceed customer expectation while making them feel like they are part of the Steiner family. Roofer employs a team of industry experts for every trade in which they work and uses only the finest in building materials for every job.

For this reason, thousands of satisfied customers have chosen Roofer for their roofing, siding, and specialty contracting needs.

What We Offer

Roofing

Your roof keeps the your family and your business safe and free from water intrusions and damage from other elements. Roofer provides residential and commercial roofing services that are reliable, efficient, and affordable.

Vinyl Siding

Vinyl siding is a great investment and can be a very important part of the appearance of your home. Our team of expert installers will help you pick out a siding that goes well with your architectural style and climate. We will also use the best quality of material to make sure your siding looks great and stays vibrant for years to come.

Specialty Contracting

The skills of the team at Roofer go far beyond your basic roofing company’s. We can help with a wide variety of specialty projects including, gutters, garage construction, deck construction, window replacement, and more. Have an extreme case? Try us and let us “wow” you.
